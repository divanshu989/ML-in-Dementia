Dementia Risk Assessment

Our advanced machine learning model helps assess the potential risk of dementia based on key neurological and demographic indicators.

What We Measure

  • Cognitive Function (MMSE)
  • Intracranial Volume (eTIV)
  • Brain Volume (nWBV)
  • Age and Education Level
  • Socioeconomic Status (SES)
  • Clinical Dementia Rating (CDR)

Important Note

This is a screening tool. Always consult with a healthcare professional for a comprehensive diagnosis.
